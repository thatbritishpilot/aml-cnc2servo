(Servo Position 8 - approximately 48 degrees)
(Binary 1000 = decimal 8)

(Set the binary pattern for position 8)
M63 P0      (Set output 0 LOW - LSB)
M63 P1      (Set output 1 LOW)
M63 P2      (Set output 2 LOW)
M62 P3      (Set output 3 HIGH - MSB)

(Dwell to allow servo to move)
G4 P0.5     (Dwell for 0.5 seconds)

(End of macro)
