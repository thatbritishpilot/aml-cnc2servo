(Servo Position 4 - approximately 45 degrees)
(Binary 0100 = decimal 4)

(Set the binary pattern for position 4)
M63 P0      (Set output 0 LOW - LSB)
M63 P1      (Set output 1 LOW)
M62 P2      (Set output 2 HIGH)
M63 P3      (Set output 3 LOW - MSB)

(Dwell to allow servo to move)
G4 P0.5     (Dwell for 0.5 seconds)

(End of macro)
