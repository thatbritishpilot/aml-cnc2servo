(Servo Position 15 - 90 degrees)
(Binary 1111 = decimal 15)

(Set the binary pattern for position 15)
M62 P0      (Set output 0 HIGH - LSB)
M62 P1      (Set output 1 HIGH)
M62 P2      (Set output 2 HIGH)
M62 P3      (Set output 3 HIGH - MSB)

(Dwell to allow servo to move)
G4 P0.5     (Dwell for 0.5 seconds)

(End of macro)
